To my fellow J-Card Template users: check your data files. Those made in Revision B and C (February 19th to 24th) are blank due to a careless mistake in the preserver. We really apologize for any inconvenience.

Contrary to what we said, we are announcing the launch of yet another web application, developed out of compassion.

Originated as a solution to a comically simple problem: viewing a local text file on a smartphone, NET-Viewer wraps the rich content-handling capabilities of your web browser into a file viewer. Its main feature is the ability to adjust the output display, like inverting its colors which can be useful when viewing PDF documents.

Based on J-Card Template, it is our first original real-mode web application (the former is a fork), and--unlike its parent--has a menu bar. Though, we admit that its control panel interface looks and feels relatively clunky.

You can access NET-Viewer in NET World. Try toying around the control panel while playing a video!

Nevertheless, we hope not to repeat this for the rest of the year.
