Recursion is a way to solve a problem by solving a smaller instance of it; divide and conquer. Observe the following procedure:

factorial passes a smaller n to itself recursively, until n is 0, with which the base case is reached. 1--the factorial for 0--is then returned to the caller, to be multiplied with its n of 1. The result is then retuned to its caller, to be multiplied with its n of 2. This continues until the original caller returns the final result.
