A goal is to keep the Online Portal accessible under a wide range of conditions--including 2G networks. Thus, we are meticulous in its use of resources. We decided that external graphics are used only for illustrative purposes, and that they must be manually-processed to reduce its size while retaining the intended amount of detail.

The Online Portal follows this set preference:

  • Unicode symbols, over
  • vectors (SVG), over
  • rasters (PNG, JPEG, WebP).
