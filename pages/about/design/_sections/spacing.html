We space elements like in a text document. After all, this is mainly an information website. HTML is originally built for our purposes, so thankfully this isn't a difficult task to begin with.

An inline margin is applied to each page to limit its width. This simulates the ratio of a text document in portrait orientation, and should reduce eye movements to help the reader stay focused on the main content.

Although we use whitespace characters as spacers in some cases, we generally avoid selectable trailing and leading whitespace characters as much as possible to provide clutter-free text. You can try selecting the entire page and inspect the highlights to verify this.
