EDEN Online was wholly hand-coded from scratch to its launch on New Year's Eve 2020 for Mozilla Firefox, with only [<{{ htmlAOut }}"/{{ site.data["home_2019-portal"].path }}">one page].

03/30/2020

Due to reports on missing margins with Safari and Chrome, its stylesheet was then backported to both browsers and others that didn't recognize bleeding-edge definitions and short forms.

04/29/2020

As the Online Portal grew, hand-coding was becoming less tolerable. So the entirety was then refactored to use [<{{ htmlAOut }}"https://jekyllrb.com" >Jekyll] in a modular fashion. However, to retain its dynamicism, content modules are still mostly hand-coded.

01/04/2021

For the same reasons as above, a metadata pool was created. This allows for any page to access the metadata of any page (including itself), greatly-easing maintenance. A page metadata documents a page's path, title, subtitle, and those of its sections.
