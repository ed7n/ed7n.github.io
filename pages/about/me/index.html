Well then. For starters, I tinker around digital audio technologies, and seldomly on imagery. I am nitpicky when it comes to media reproduction. I thrift around for retro portable audio junk. Usually, I enjoy having things done professionally. Much of what I have dealt with are the total opposite: I broke self-service kiosks, everyday computer programs, and even (sloppily-setup) airport public computers.

Hmm, I guess that's enough for now, if you want to socialize, then drop me an email and—depending on my state—we'll start from there. Oh, and thanks for reading.
