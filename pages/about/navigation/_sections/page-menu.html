The page menu allows jumping to individual sections of a page, or to the logical-parent page. Occassionally, menu item labels are limited to two short or one long word. In which case, a label doesn't always reflect its actual referenced title.

The location of the page menu depends on the current document layout:

  • Standard and printUnder the header.
  • MobileTap on floating button labelled "≡ Menu" at the bottom-left of the visible area.
