Page Menu

Each page comes with a menu of links to its sections and logical parents. Its labels may be limited to two short or one long word, so they do not always reflect the actual titles of their references.

The location of the page menu depends on the current [{%include a-page.html key="_about_layouts" html="document layout"%}]:

  • Standard, mini!, or PrintUnder the header; scroll up and look for {{navLabelPage}}.
  • MobileTap on the {{navLabelTree}} button at the bottom-left of the visible area.

Jump Menu

The bottom of a page lies the jump menu of links to the home page and others that we found central.
