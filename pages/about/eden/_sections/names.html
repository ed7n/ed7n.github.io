On its own, the name ED7 has no meaning outside its identifier.

It originated in 2004 when I first came up with ED [iːdi] for no logical reason. 7 was then appended in 2007 as it was my favorite number at the time. I discovered that you can concatenate the third, forth, and fifth characters of my name ("end"), spell the resulting word out loud, and thus you just uttered E and D!

In 2013, it came to my senses that the name ED7 ENTERTAINMENT is too long, and that a lot of people were mistaken with the pronunciation of ED7 as [ɛd sɛvən] "add seven" instead of [iːdi sɛvən] "E.D. seven." So I abbreviated the whole name to EDEN by taking the first two characters of each word. This is indeed pronounced like the familiar English word "eden."
