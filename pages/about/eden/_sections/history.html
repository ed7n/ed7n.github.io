We were founded in late 2008 with the launch of long lost Shoot Brendon, an interactive PowerPoint "game" in which the player taps on my face in each slide to proceed, or else the game ends. It was my first ever publicized media (within my classmates), and I wanted to put a pseudo-brand on it for identification purposes.

We were once focused solely on media production; that is no longer the case once I diverted into programming in the mid 2010s. Today, EDEN is simply an alternative identifier to myself in my projects.

It shouldn't be a surprise that I've been running EDEN all by myself for over a decade.
