We were founded in late 2008 with the launch of long lost Shoot Brendon, an interactive PowerPoint "game" in which the player taps on my face to proceed to the next slide. It was my first ever publicized media (within classmates), and I wanted to put an aspiring pseudo-brand on it.

We were once focused solely on media production; that is no longer the case once I diverted into programming in the mid 2010s. Today, EDEN is an alternative identifier to myself in my projects.

It shouldn't be a surprise that I've been running EDEN by myself for over a decade.
