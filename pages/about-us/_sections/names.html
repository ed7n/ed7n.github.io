ED7 is purely an identifier and has no meaning otherwise. It originated in 2004 as ED [iːdi], then appended with 7 in 2007 as it was my favorite number at the time.

Due to the lengthiness of {{siteData.orgName}}, and the unintentional pronunciation of ED7 as add seven [ɛd sɛvən] instead of E.D. seven [iːdi sɛvən], {{siteData.orgNameShort}} was created in 2013 with first two characters of each word. It is pronounced like the English word eden.
