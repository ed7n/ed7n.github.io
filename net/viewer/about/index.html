Despite the spectrum of rich content web browsers are capable of handling, their interface--particularly their mobile counterpart--as a file viewer is lacking. {{data["viewer"].body.title}} wraps said capabilities with our frontend primarily to provide some adjustments of the output.

{{data["viewer"].body.title}} supports loading audio, image, and video files; HTML and PDF documents; while those otherwise are displayed as plain text. Note that support ultimately depends on your browser.

{{data["viewer"].body.title}} derived from [{%include a-page.html key= "jcard-template"%}] as a second testbed to generalize our web application infrastructure, and use the [{%include a-page.html key="unagi"%}] menu bar in practice for the first time.
