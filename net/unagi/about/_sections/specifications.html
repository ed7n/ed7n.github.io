Form Fields

Unagi works on most sizes, the smallest being the iPhone 5 or SE. It offers 20* field styles optimized for either desktop- or mobile-class applications. So while the ideal designer would cherry-pick as they see fit, Unagi employs a common design language such that switching between styles would be comfortable to both developers and end-users.

Menu Bar

On top of form fields, a desktop-class menu bar is available. Its mobile-class equivalent is yet to come.

Minimum Required Width

Using the Cantarell typeface and a global font size of 11pt, a bare presentation of Unagi requires a container of at least 271px wide, 286px if contained within a fieldset. An ideal presentation--like those in the [demonstration]--requires a viewport of at least 310px, which is met with the iPhone 5 or SE (320px). These requirement can be brought down with the exclusive use of small fields.

Technologies

Unagi uses only internal components except JavaScript**, thus not relying on external libraries and resources.

*4 field styles by 5 input styles.

**though an ideal application would want to use JavaScript to enhance its usability.
