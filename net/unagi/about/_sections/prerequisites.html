For Unagi, we follow a policy of continuous advancements in software technologies. Therefore, legacy or deprecated user agents--such as Internet Explorer 11--are not supported.

Unagi is designed mainly with Firefox (Gecko)*, but is tested to be working on Chromium (Blink) and Epiphany (WebKit). We guess that any one that's based on one of those three and is at most five years of age should work**. The oldest one Unagi has been tested against is WebView on Android 9 (Chrome/79.0.3945.116). Note that Google Chrome is based on Chromium, and Safari uses the WebKit browser engine.

*that is rigorously-configured to have broken some websites.

**While we urge users update their regular web browser to benefit from enhancements and corrections, we'd like to urge designers of web browsers not to introduce changes that could potentially leak salt onto its users, such as the transition from Fennec to Fenix.
