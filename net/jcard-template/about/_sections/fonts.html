Any font supported by your browser can be used. As far as I know, there are no web APIs that reliably-return a list of available fonts. You may retrieve such a list in your browser settings.

For your browser's defaults, use either serif, sans-serif, monospace, cursive, fantasy, math, emoji, or fangsong. Your browser may not support the last five values.

If fonts are not changing, you may want to consider the following:

  • Your browser does not recognize the entered font name.
    • Verify that a font with this name is installed, and that your browser recognizes it.
      • You may have to restart your browser after installing a font for it to be recognized.
  • Custom fonts is disabled.
    • Enable said setting in your browser--without fear of additional Internet usage.
