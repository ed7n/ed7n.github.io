REM or remark statements do not make part of the layout metadata, but are used for annotations. They can be placed anywhere in a cuesheet.

However, when parsed into QEdit, they will be placed together into their respective groups in the same order as they are found. Specifically:

  • REMs before the first TRACK will be placed at the top of the cuesheet.
  • REMs after TRACK and before INDEX will be placed after FLAGS and before CD-Text statements and PREGAP, in that order.
  • REMs after INDEX and before another INDEX will be placed as they are found.
  • REMs after INDEX and before TRACK will be placed after POSTGAP and before FILE.
